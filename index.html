Dear readers! In the US, a unique patented device for the treatment and healing of wounds - Qoustic Wound Therapy System™ from Arobella Medical.

Despite all the achievements of modern medicine, the number of patients with purulent-inflammatory diseases (for example, complications after surgery, with diabetes) is not decreasing. With all the variety of methods available to doctors, treatment of purulent wounds is characterized by long periods and a high cost of treatment.

In the US, purulent wounds cause 330,000 hospitalizations a year. However, with such diseases as necrotic infections of soft tissues, a high percentage of deaths is recorded. Treatment is complicated by the fact that at present pathogenic organisms are quite resistant to many antibiotics.

That is why the value and uniqueness of the Qoustic Wound Therapy System™ is difficult to overestimate. The number of diseases under which it is successfully applied is impressive and gives hope to many desperate patients.

The Qoustic Wound Therapy System™ is designed to aid the wound healing process for a variety of wounds, including

  • Pressure/Diabetic
  • Chronic wounds
  • Infected wounds
  • Biofilm
  • Burns
  • Superficial

    • Low-frequency ultrasound therapy is indicated for surgical infection to treat purulent soft tissue diseases and burns of any degree. Qoustic Wound Therapy System ™ gives excellent results in bedsores, eczematous wounds, tropical ulcers, fistulas, as well as diseases of the "Diabetic foot"; Successfully used to prepare a wound bed for a graft or other subsequent procedures and surgical treatment of a wound.
